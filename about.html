The Journey

From Boutique Firm to Global Powerhouse

Founded in 1998 by Alexander Sterling in a small office in Lower Manhattan, Stackly began with a singular vision: to bring institutional-grade financial strategy to the mid-market sector.

Over the decades, we have weathered three major recessions, creating a methodology that prioritizes resilience over rapid, unstable growth. Today, we advise over 500 enterprise clients across 40 countries, managing a collective portfolio valuation of over $40 Billion.

We believe that true wealth is not just about accumulation, but about architectural structural integrity.

1998 Foundation Stackly opens its doors in New York City with a team of three.
2005 London Expansion Opened our first European headquarters to serve EMEA clients.
2012 Tech Integration Launched proprietary AI risk modeling software, setting a new industry standard.
2024 Global Summit Celebrated 25 years of excellence and crossed $40B in assets advised.
