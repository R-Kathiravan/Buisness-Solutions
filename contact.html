Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum engagement size?
We typically work with enterprises with annual revenues exceeding $10M. However, we do offer specialized packages for high-growth startups in our "Venture" program.
Do you offer remote consultations?
Yes. While we prefer initial kick-off meetings to be in-person at one of our global hubs, 80% of our ongoing advisory work is conducted via secure digital channels.
How is billing structured?
We offer both retainer-based models for ongoing strategy and project-based pricing for specific audits or legal restructurings. Contact us for a custom quote.
Are you hiring?
We are always looking for exceptional talent in finance, law, and data science. Please visit our Careers page or email your CV to talent@stackly.com.
